Survive.web

Welcome to Survive.web, the decision making quiz where you can test your mettle during the inevitable zombie apocalypse. Before you play, let's get you acquainted with the rules and game instructions:


  • You will be presented with 10 zombie apocalypse scenarios and questions.
  • For each scenario and question, you will have four options to choose from.
  • The options have a score value ranging from 10 to 40. When you select an option, your total score will increase.
  • Everytime you select an option, the next scenario will be displayed.
  • At the end of the game, your score will be assessed and depending on the score you acheive, we will decide how likely you are to survive.
  • There is background music that will play throughout the course of the game, you can mute this by using the mute button.
  • If you reload the page for any reason, you will be returned to this screen and you will have to begin again.
